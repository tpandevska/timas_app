Our Story

TIMAS DOOEL exists since 1993. Our business started as a small market selling various everyday products to the people in the neighborhood. Not too long after that we bought our first machine and started manufacturing aluminium foil.

With a lot of ambition, motivation and hard work we became one of the leading companies specialized in manufacturing and selling various products for everyday use in households, restaurants, schools, coffee shops and much more.

TIMAS is engaged in the production, wholesale and retail of disposable products.
Manufacturer of: aluminum foil, stretch foil, baking paper, napkins, wipers, complex wipers, toilet, composite toilet and paper trays. We also offer a wide range of catering utensils and plastic utensils for home and commercial use.

Our Mission

In addition to the quality of the products, it is important for us to keep the low price of our products and the favorable payment terms.

We are a mission-driven company that aims to set the standards of excellence. We are building a business in which high standards permeate all aspects of our company. Quality is a state of mind at TIMAS.
